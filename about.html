About Sagar Ratan Foundation

Founded in 2025, we are a community-focused NGO delivering relief and long-term development in education, nutrition, and health access.

Our Focus Areas

Food Security

Meal distribution, nutrition packs, and community kitchens.

Health Outreach

Free medical checkups, medicines, and health awareness drives.

Education Support

Scholarships, digital kits, and study centers.
